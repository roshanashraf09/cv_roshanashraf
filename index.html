The Kebab
of the Future.

Forget what you thought you knew. Born in Berlin and perfected over three decades, GDK took the humble doner and turned it into a premium dining experience.

We use only the finest whole-cut meats, never compressed. Our bread is freshly baked. Our kitchens are open — because we have nothing to hide.
